Z.ai Enhancer is installed

A floating panel that makes chat.z.ai more powerful.

Language

Choose the extension's language. This applies to the panel, toasts, and this welcome page.

What you get

  • 🔊
    Sound + toast on completion

    Get an audible chime and a small toast when the agent finishes responding.

  • 📨
    Auto-send queue

    Schedule a sequence of messages.

  • 📚
    Prompt library

    Save reusable prompts with {{variables}}.

  • 📊
    Response stats

    See the average duration of recent responses.

How to use it

  1. Open chat.z.ai and sign in.
  2. You'll see a circular button (FAB) on the right side (vertically centered).
  3. The panel has 4 tabs: Notifications, Prompts, Auto-Send, and Settings.
  4. You can also use keyboard shortcuts (see below).

Keyboard shortcuts

Action Shortcut
Open the panel on chat.z.aiCtrl+Shift+Z
Toggle completion soundAlt+Shift+S
Click the Stop buttonAlt+Shift+X

On macOS, use Cmd instead of Ctrl.
